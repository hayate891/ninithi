Electrical Properties of Single-Wall Carbon Nanotubes

Carbon nanotubes exhibit remarkable electrical properties and therefore widely used in nanotechnology research. To view the band structure of a nanotube check the show electrical properties checkbox in the nanotube input panel. 

You can change the parameters using the panel on the right side and plot the graph again
